Welcome! To start weaving on the your newly assembled loom, follow this five step process:
  1. Choose the number of shafts (s)
  2. Choose the number of pedals (p)
    3. These options are located in the "Weaving Draft Operations" Subpanel.
  3. Experiment with various configurations for the threading matrix, pressing the Send New Threading button once you are finished.
  4. Experiment with various configurations for the tie-up and treadling matrices, respectively, until you reach a desired pattern.
  5. Begin weaving with the Next Row and Previous Row buttons, throwing your weft shuttle in the shed between every timestep!
