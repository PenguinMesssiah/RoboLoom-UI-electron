Calibration Mode

Automatic Calibration

In order to Automatically Calibrate the Jaquard Loom, you must ensure the following:
* ALL the motor heddles are set to the DOWN position.
* Your computer is physically connected to your Loom's Arduino with an open Serial port.

Manual Calibration

Use manual calibration in the event ANY of the Jaquard Loom's heddles are not in the DOWN position
