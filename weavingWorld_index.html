World of Weaving | History & Culture

Overview

Welcome to the World of Weaving, an interactive tool to discover, learn, and practice historically and culturally relevant weaving patterns.

How to Use This Tool

This tool arranges weaving patterns by their respective geographic region of origin. To explore these traditional patterns and the indigenous communities who produced them, simply click on the desired map region to read about their society and contributions to the field of weaving!
